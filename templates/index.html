Important Medical Disclaimer

This tool is for informational and educational purposes only. It is NOT a medical diagnostic tool, does NOT provide medical advice, and should NOT be used for self-diagnosis or to make treatment decisions.

The classification results are based on text analysis and may not accurately reflect your actual mental state. Many factors influence mental health that cannot be determined from text alone.

If you are experiencing any of the following, please seek immediate help:

  • Thoughts of suicide or self-harm
  • Plans to hurt yourself or others
  • Severe depression or anxiety that interferes with daily life
  • Sudden mood changes or loss of touch with reality

Crisis Resources (Available 24/7):

  • Suicide Prevention (Sweden): Call 90101 or visit mind.se
  • Healthcare Advice (Sweden): Call 1177
  • Emergency (Sweden): Call 112
  • International Crisis Lines: findahelpline.com

For non-emergency mental health support: Please consult with a licensed mental health professional such as a psychologist, psychiatrist, or counselor.
