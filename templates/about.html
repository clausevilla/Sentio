About Sentio

Understanding the science and technology behind our AI-powered mental health text analysis system

Our Mission

Our Mental Health Analysis System was developed as part of an AI research project to help individuals better understand their mental health through text analysis. The system leverages natural language processing and machine learning techniques to identify 4 different mental health states in written communication: Normal, Depression, Suicidal and Stress.

We believe that early awareness of mental health patterns can lead to better outcomes. By providing accessible, AI-powered analysis tools, we aim to help people recognize mental health indicators in their communication and take proactive steps toward seeking appropriate support when needed.

The Technology

Training Data

The underlying model was trained on a comprehensive mental health dataset from multiple social media platforms including Reddit, Twitter, and other sources. This dataset contains labeled examples of four mental health states, providing real-world examples of how people express different mental health conditions in their communication.

Four Mental Health Categories

Our model classifies text into one of four mental health states, each with specific characteristics:

  • Normal: Balanced emotional state with no significant mental health indicators
  • Depression: Language reflecting sadness, hopelessness, loss of interest, and low energy
  • Suicidal: Critical indicators of suicidal ideation requiring immediate attention
  • Stress: Expressions of feeling overwhelmed, pressured, or unable to cope
