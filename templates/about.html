About Sentio

Understanding the science and technology behind our AI-powered mental health text analysis system

Our Mission

Our Mental Health Analysis System was developed as part of an AI research project to help individuals better understand their mental health through text analysis. The system leverages natural language processing and machine learning techniques to identify seven different mental health states in written communication: Normal, Depression, Suicidal thoughts, Anxiety, Stress, Bi-Polar patterns, and Personality Disorder indicators.

We believe that early awareness of mental health patterns can lead to better outcomes. By providing accessible, AI-powered analysis tools, we aim to help people recognize mental health indicators in their communication and take proactive steps toward seeking appropriate support when needed.

The Technology

Training Data

The underlying model was trained on a comprehensive mental health dataset from multiple social media platforms including Reddit, Twitter, and other sources. This dataset contains labeled examples of seven mental health states, providing real-world examples of how people express different mental health conditions in their communication.

Seven Mental Health Categories

Our model classifies text into one of seven mental health states, each with specific characteristics:

  • Normal: Balanced emotional state with no significant mental health indicators
  • Depression: Language reflecting sadness, hopelessness, loss of interest, and low energy
  • Suicidal: Critical indicators of suicidal ideation requiring immediate attention
  • Anxiety: Worry-related language, fear, uncertainty, and restlessness
  • Stress: Expressions of feeling overwhelmed, pressured, or unable to cope
  • Bi-Polar: Language patterns suggesting mood fluctuations or manic/depressive episodes
  • Personality Disorder: Communication patterns associated with personality disorder characteristics

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. All data is securely stored and encrypted. We never share your personal information or analysis results with third parties. Only you can access your stress analysis history.

If you choose to create an account, you can track your progress over time. However, the system also works without an account for one-time analyses.

Who Can Benefit

Our stress detection system is designed for:

  • Individuals: Gain awareness of mental health patterns in your written communication and track changes over time
  • Mental Health Awareness: Gain insights into emotional patterns and stress indicators
  • Researchers: Explore the application of NLP in mental health analysis
  • Students & Educators: Learn about AI applications in healthcare and wellbeing

This tool is for informational and educational purposes only. It is NOT a medical diagnostic tool, does NOT provide medical advice, and should NOT be used for self-diagnosis or to make treatment decisions.

The classification results are based on text analysis and may not accurately reflect your actual mental health state. Many factors influence mental health that cannot be determined from text alone.
