Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Important Notice: This is a student project prototype developed for educational purposes. While we implement privacy best practices, this application is not intended for processing real personal mental health data. If you are experiencing mental health concerns, please consult with qualified healthcare professionals.

1. Introduction

Sentio is an AI-powered mental health text analysis tool created as an educational project. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including sensitive mental health data processed by our application.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Sentio, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and encrypted password when you register for an account.
  • Mental Health Text Data: The text content you submit for analysis through our mental health detection system.
  • Analysis Results: The AI-generated mental health state classifications and predictions based on your submitted text.
  • Usage Data: Timestamps of when you submit text for analysis, your registration date, and your last login.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your user account and enable authentication.
  • Mental Health Analysis: To process your text through our machine learning model and provide mental health state predictions.
  • History Tracking: To store your analysis results so you can review your mental health trends over time.
  • Service Improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve the accuracy of our analysis system (for educational purposes only).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. Your mental health information remains private and is used solely within the Sentio application.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your sensitive mental health data based on your explicit consent. Before using our analysis features, you will be asked to provide informed consent to the collection and processing of your mental health text data. You have the right to withdraw this consent at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • Password Protection: User passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptographic algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
  • Access Controls: Each user can only access their own data. Administrative access is restricted and logged.
  • Database Security: Our database implements authentication and access controls to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user of Sentio, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your mental health analysis records in your account history.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export and download all your personal data in a machine-readable json format.
  • Right to Erasure: You can permanently delete all your mental health analysis data or delete your entire account at any time through your profile settings.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by deleting your data or account.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Stored until you choose to delete your data or account.
  • Mental Health Analysis Records: Stored until you choose to delete all your data.
  • Deleted Data: When you delete your data or account, all associated information is permanently removed from our database.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Sentio uses minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We use Django's session framework to maintain your logged-in state. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics services that would share your data with external parties.

9. Data Transfers

This is a student project prototype. If deployed, data would be stored on servers . We do not transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area without implementing appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR.

10. Children's Privacy

Sentio is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are under 18, please do not use this service or provide any personal information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal data, you can contact us.

13. Educational Purpose Disclaimer

This application is developed as a student project for educational purposes. While we implement privacy best practices and GDPR principles, this is not a production system certified for processing real sensitive health data. The mental health analysis provided by this tool is generated by a machine learning model for demonstration purposes only and should not be used as a substitute for professional mental health assessment or treatment.
